Our Story

About Varta Homes

A family-founded firm with a simple principle: build every project as if your name is on it — because ours is.

Since 2009

Built from the ground up.

Varta Homes was founded in 2009 by a second-generation builder who grew up on job sites. From a single custom home in Barrie, we grew into one of Ontario's most respected construction and development firms — without ever losing the care and attention of a family business.

We don't chase volume. We pursue quality — and the two rarely coexist at scale. That's why we limit the number of active projects we take on at any given time, so every client gets the full attention of our senior team.

Over 15 years, we've completed more than 240 projects across residential, commercial, and mixed-use sectors. Our repeat client rate — above 60% — tells you everything about how we operate.
